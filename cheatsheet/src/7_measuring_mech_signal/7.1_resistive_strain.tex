\subsubsection*{Bulk Conductors}
\textbf{Young's modulus:}

\[
E
=
\frac{\sigma}{\varepsilon}
=
\frac{F/A_0}{\Delta l / l_0}, \qquad
A = \pi r^2, \qquad
R = \rho \frac{l}{A}
\]
\textbf{Gauge factor:}\\
Assuming volume conservation
\(
A\,l = \text{const.}
\),
one obtains:
\[
GF
=
\frac{\Delta R / R_0}{\Delta l / l_0}, \qquad
\frac{\Delta R}{R_0}
=
2\,\frac{\Delta l}{l_0},\qquad
\boxed{
GF = 2
}
\]
