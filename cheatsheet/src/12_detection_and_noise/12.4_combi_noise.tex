\subsection{Combination of Noise Sources}

Noise sources are uncorrelated
$\;\Rightarrow\;$
noise powers add.

\textbf{Total signal-to-noise ratio:}
\[
\left(\frac{S}{N}\right)^2
=
\frac{i_{\text{sig}}^2}
{\langle i_n^2\rangle_{\text{photon}}
+ \langle i_n^2\rangle_{\text{Johnson}}
+ \langle i_n^2\rangle_{\text{dark}}
+ \langle i_n^2\rangle_{\text{flicker}}}
\]

\textbf{Power scaling:}
Signal current $\propto P$,
photon shot noise $\propto \sqrt{P}$.
